Our Services

Amplify your message and enhance your design with our print methods

Sublimation

Sublimation Printing is a form of digital printing that combines with heat, pressure and transfer technique. The printing output is almost permanent, high resolution and full colour printing can be done.

Silkscreen Printing

Silkscreen Printing is a stenciling method that involves printing ink through stencils that are supported by a porous fabric mesh stretched across a frame called a screen.

Heatpress

Heatpress Printing is a machine engineered to imprint a design or graphic on a substrate, such as a t-shirt, with the application of heat and pressure for a preset period of time.

Machine Embroidery

Machine Embroidery is a form of computerized technique using embroidery software to do stitching on top of materials. Embroidery is usually done on fabric materials such as bags, caps, T-Shirts, Blankets.
